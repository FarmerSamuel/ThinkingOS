## Informative foundations

- Socratic questioning as a method for testing definitions, assumptions, evidence, alternatives, and consequences.
- Problem-framing practices that separate desired outcomes, observed conditions, causal hypotheses, constraints, and proposed solutions.
- Hypothesis testing principles that treat alternative explanations and disconfirming evidence as necessary to calibrated conclusions.

Informative references guide design but do not override the normative ThinkingOS contracts. Future external citations should include stable provenance and version or publication information.
