# Universal Output Schema

Every skill should map its result to the following Markdown-compatible structure. Sections may be brief, but their meaning must remain consistent.

## Summary

The direct answer, conclusion, or current status in decision-ready language.

## Evaluation

The result of applying the skill's criteria, including important strengths, weaknesses, trade-offs, or alternatives.

## Reasoning

The evidence, assumptions, and logical connections supporting the evaluation. Provide an understandable rationale without relying on authority.

## Missing Information

Unknowns or ambiguities that could materially affect the result. Use `None identified` when appropriate.

## Next Recommendation

The single most useful next action, question, or ThinkingOS skill for advancing the validated goal.

## Confidence

A calibrated level—**High**, **Medium**, or **Low**—with a brief explanation based on evidence quality, completeness, and uncertainty.
