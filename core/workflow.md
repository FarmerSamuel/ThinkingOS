# Universal Workflow

```text
Input
  ↓
Validate
  ↓
Critique
  ↓
Logic Test
  ↓
Decision
  ↓
Output
  ↓
Next Thinking Skill
```

## Stages

1. **Input:** Capture the user's goal, context, claims, evidence, constraints, and desired outcome without silently adding assumptions.
2. **Validate:** Check completeness, ambiguity, source quality, relevance, and whether the stated problem is the real problem.
3. **Critique:** Challenge assumptions, expose blind spots, consider alternatives, and distinguish facts from interpretations.
4. **Logic Test:** Evaluate consistency, causal claims, dependencies, trade-offs, and whether conclusions follow from the available evidence.
5. **Decision:** Identify the best-supported conclusion or decision boundary. If the evidence is insufficient, decide what must be learned next instead of guessing.
6. **Output:** Present a structured, transparent result using the universal output schema.
7. **Next Thinking Skill:** Recommend the next reusable thinking capability only when it materially advances the current goal.

Stages may loop when validation or logic testing reveals missing or contradictory information.
