# Philosophy

ThinkingOS is a reusable, AI-agnostic thinking framework—not a prompt collection. It defines durable reasoning habits that can be applied across models, tools, domains, and skills.

## Core beliefs

1. **Think before solving.** A fast answer to the wrong problem creates false progress.
2. **Validate assumptions.** Important claims should be supported, tested, or clearly labeled as uncertain.
3. **Separate definition from generation.** Establish the problem, goal, scope, constraints, and evidence before proposing solutions.
4. **Make thinking reusable.** Validation, critique, logic testing, decision framing, and reflection should transfer between use cases.
5. **Improve human thinking.** AI should increase clarity, rigor, and awareness of trade-offs rather than replace human judgment or accountability.

ThinkingOS therefore treats uncertainty, questions, and revisions as productive parts of reasoning—not failures to produce an immediate answer.
