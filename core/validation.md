# Validation Framework

Every ThinkingOS skill must validate its inputs before analysis. Validation is a gate, not a final disclaimer.

## Required checks

### Inputs

- Confirm the input matches the skill's declared purpose and expected form.
- Identify relevant goals, constraints, evidence, sources, and success criteria.

### Missing information

- Determine which unknowns could materially change the analysis or recommendation.
- Ask for blocking information; record non-blocking gaps in the output.

### Ambiguity

- Detect terms, scope, ownership, timeframes, or desired outcomes with multiple plausible meanings.
- Clarify material ambiguity rather than choosing a meaning silently.

### Logical consistency

- Check claims and constraints for contradictions.
- Verify that premises support the requested inference and that evidence is relevant to the conclusion.

## Validation result

Validation must produce one of three outcomes: **ready**, **ready with disclosed assumptions**, or **needs clarification**. Analysis proceeds only when the result is ready or safely ready with assumptions.
